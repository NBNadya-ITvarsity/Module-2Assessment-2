WISH

MOVIE INFO:

Name of movie: Wish

Genre : Animated, Comedy, Family

Age Restriction: PG V


In the Kingdom of Rosas, located off of the Iberian Peninsula, a 17-year-old girl named Asha senses a darkness that no one else does about the kingdom's ruler, King Magnifico. This eventually leads to her trying to make a passionate plea to the stars in a moment of need. Soon, an actual star from the sky named Star answers Asha's wish. After the star falls from the sky, it is revealed that the star has magical powers to grant wishes as well. Cast Ariana DeBose, Chris Pine, Angelique Cabral, Alan Tudyk Directed by Chris Buck, Fawn Veerasunthorn
